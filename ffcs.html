Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS)

The Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS) is an innovative academic model designed to give you significant control over your learning journey. It allows you to choose your courses, faculty members, and even your class timings based on your preferences and availability. This system offers unparalleled flexibility, enabling you to build a personalized timetable that best suits your learning style and extracurricular commitments. With FFCS, you're empowered to manage your academic load and progress at your own pace, making your college experience truly adaptable.

Key Benefits:

  • Personalized Timetable: Select classes that fit your schedule.
  • Faculty Choice: Choose professors based on reviews and teaching styles.
  • Self-Paced Learning: Manage your credit load each semester.
  • Optimal Learning Environment: Tailor your education to your needs.
